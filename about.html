My Background

Educational Journey

I am a dedicated student pursuing a Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Metallurgical & Materials Engineering at the National Institute of Technology (NIT), Raipur. With a strong foundation in science and mathematics from my higher secondary education at Techno India Group Academia, I have developed a deep interest in understanding the properties, behavior, and applications of materials.

Academic Achievements

Throughout my academic career, I have consistently focused on combining theoretical knowledge with practical application. My coursework includes advanced topics in materials science, crystallography, thermodynamics, and materials processing. I actively participate in laboratory sessions to gain hands-on experience with sophisticated testing and analysis equipment.

Experience & Expertise

Laboratory Work

I have gained extensive experience in metallurgical laboratory practices, including optical microscopy, scanning electron microscopy (SEM), X-ray diffraction (XRD), and mechanical testing. I am proficient in preparing metallographic samples, conducting tensile tests, hardness tests, and impact tests following industry standards and guidelines.

Research Interests

My research interests span several areas including microstructural analysis of metals and alloys, phase transformations, corrosion behavior, material degradation, and the development of advanced materials. I am particularly interested in understanding how material composition and processing conditions affect mechanical and physical properties.

Professional Development

Beyond academics, I am committed to continuous learning and professional growth. I actively seek opportunities to attend seminars, workshops, and conferences related to metallurgy and materials science. I believe in the importance of staying updated with the latest developments and innovations in the field.
